ERNI Foosball rules

That this application relies on.
  • One match is played until one of the teams has 2 won games.
  • The game is played until one of the teams reaches 8 goals.
  • No "grilling" is allowed.
  • The first "kick off" is done from the hole in the center.
  • When the team gets a goal the ball is played from the corner of the field.
  • If the ball leaves the table it is played from the hole in the center.
  • If the ball stops between two teams the ball must be played from the hole in the center.
  • If the ball stops between the players of one team it can be moved by the player himself.
  • When one team scores and the ball jumps out of the goal the opponent must subtract one point from his score.
